## Getting Started

CRISP is a collaborative platform for course management, code analysis, and team-based learning. Follow these steps to get started:

- **Register:** Create an account via the Sign up button and wait for admin approval.
- **Sign in:** Once approved, sign in with your credentials to access your dashboard.
- **Join a course:** Your instructor will add you to a course. You can view your assigned courses on the Courses page.

## Key Features

### Course Overview

View course timelines, assessments, and your progress at a glance. Navigate between different course views from the sidebar.

### Assessments

Complete coding assignments, submit your work, and track feedback. Internal assessments and peer reviews help reinforce your learning.

### Teams & Repositories

Collaborate with your team on shared repositories. View PR overviews, code analysis results, and team analytics.

### Timeline

Stay on track with the course timeline. See upcoming deadlines and milestones for all your assessments and deliverables.
